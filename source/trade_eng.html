Trading modules

Modules of trade are established exclusively on transport ships. The following modules are available:

  • Cargo Bay Extension
  • Shipment Computer
  • Rush
  • Trade Boost
  • Trade Burst
  • Shipment Drone
  • Shipment Beam
  • Offload
  • Entrust
  • Recall
  • Dispatch

Shipment Drone

The transport ship launches a drone, which delivers the goods automatically. The drone can only move between sectors connected by active warp lines. Each drone takes cargo to only one destination. When unloading, you will receive additional credits for each delivered cargo in excess of one (the first). The award will increase proportionally depending on the number of goods. The speed of the drone is 1320 ae/m Blueprints are available at the 5+ level of the Red Star.

Dispatch

The Transport ship initiates teleport to the planet with the most items (Shipments, Artifacts or Relics) in the star system. If more than one planets have the same amount of items, the farthest planet is picked. The Teleport completes after the preparation time elapses. The preparation time is proportional to the amount of items on the target planet at the time of activation. Blueprints are available on 6+ the RS level.
