Mining modules

  • Hydrogen bay extension
  • Mining boost
  • Enrich
  • Mass mining
  • Hydrogen unload
  • Mining unity
  • Crunch
  • Genesis
  • Mining drone
  • HydroRocket

Mining drone

An automated mining ship that targets a single asteroid. Once mining is complete, the drone will deliver the Hydrogen to the closest planet and self-destruct. It does not work in sectors with jump gates to white stars. Level 9 is a blue crystal.

General data for all levels:
Range - 600 AU
The lifetime: 1 h.in RS, 25 d. in WS
Cooldown: 1 h.in RS, 25 d. in WS
The flight speed: 1200 AU/m in RS,120AU/h in WS

HydroRocket

Allows the Miner ship to launch a powerful Rocket, anywhere in the star system. To activate, the Miner ship must have collected at least the Required Hydrogen amount. The Hydrogen is consumed on activation. A powerful Rocket launched by a Miner ship. The Rocket explodes and does Area damage to all enemy ships when it reaches its destination. Enemy ships can fire on the Rocket using normal targeting rules. No damage is applied if the rocket is destroyed before it reaches its destination. Level 8 is a blue crystal.

General data for all levels:
Speed - 1380 AU/m
Range - 120 AU
Cooldown: - 60sec
Activation cost (hydrogen on Board) - 500
