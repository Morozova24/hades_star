Planets

Found in systems of yellow stars of the planet, belong to inhabited worlds which can be colonized by the player for the purpose of receiving the income in the form of hydrogen and game currency, and also every hour generate freights for which shipment the player receives remuneration in the form of game currency. The amount of reward for cargo depends on the level of the planet. On the planets, and the satellites of the planets, can be stored artifacts, one artifact occupies one cargo position, regardless of its level. Planets are divided into terrestrial, desert, gas giants, etc., and inside the species have a level that is determined by the characteristics of the speed of delivery of goods, maximum improvement, credit growth. Players can exchange artifacts of the method of unloading-loading them on the planets. For example, to transfer artifacts to another player in the system of Red Stars. Typically for simplicity at level 1. Planets found in sectors of star systems can be colonized only if one of the ships is at the planet. Colonization time is always instantaneous. It is noteworthy that after improving the planet to 17 ur, you will be able to observe the movement of the station in its orbit. At the moment it is possible to open up to 16 planets, and each of them to improve to 40 levels.

To calculate the income from the delivery of goods of a particular planet, it is enough to multiply the basic income CR of the level (X) by the modifier of the planet type (Y) (column modifier of income from the cargo from the table of planet open). For an hourly income, the resulting value is divided by 24. To automatically calculate the income from the goods and the size of the storage limit of Your system, use the calculation calculator by selecting it in the main menu of the application.
