Sentinel

Sentinels have weak hulls but are equipped with a very powerful Battery. They pose little threat individually, but are deadly in large numbers or when covered behind stronger Cerberus ships. Sentinels will always stay within their assigned sector. Sentinels are the first type of Cerberus ships encountered in game. These ships mostly appear in ones and twos in Level 1-2 RS, but they will spawn in much greater numbers later on. Also, when strategically positioned behind a Guardian (see below), multiple Sentinels are dangerous to Battleships mounting only single-target weapons, like Battery or Laser. Strategy must be used, especially later in-game, when dealing with these ships.

Guardian

Guardians are stronger (in terms of hull) to Sentinels, but possess a much weaker battery. Alone these ships are not a problem, but when there are Sentinels hiding behind them, this can increase the danger exponentially. The problem is their toughness, they are well suited to taking hits for Sentinels, which can whittle down the health of your Battleships. Use strategy to clear out the Sentinels first if you can.

Interceptor

The interceptor, appearing at the level of RS4+, is a dangerous ship that is different from any other ship encountered in the previous RS. It is equipped with a volley battery which allows to conduct fight with 4 purposes, and it is not limited by a zone of action in the sector. The interceptor becomes active since the invasion in the sector of deployment of any ship, regardless of where the endpoint of the movement of the ship in the sector or outside it. After activation Interceptor, begins the pursuit of the invading ship in the sector, and in case of loss of the target, begins to attack all targets in the system, starting with the nearest, mainly pursuing the initiator. The interceptor will not shoot at transport and mining ships owned by players who did not activate them.

Colossus

Colossus, introduced from KZ5, behaves like a guardian, does not leave his sector. In fact, he is the "elder brother" of the Guardian, he is distinguished by the high strength of the case, having a strong protection and laser on board. It is recommended that all the auxiliary ships, Guardians and Guardians be destroyed in the Colossus sector, after which the Colossus, left without support, is easily destroyed by 3 Battleships using support modules. With the destruction of other ships in the sector, the Colossus's corps is partially restored.

CERBERUS BOMBER

A strong ship that regularly fires one rocket towards each participating player that has ships in the bomber's sector or any adjacent sectors. This ship will always stay within its assigned sector, but the Rockets can be fired on approaching ships in adjacent sectors.Each Rocket travels towards the enemy player's closest ship. Tries to stay away from the player in the sector. Appears at the level of 7+ Red Stars.
